NYC Congestion Pricing — Equity & Mode Shift

Pulled 509M taxi trips, 93M subway records, and Census income data to study who gets hurt by NYC's congestion toll. Ride-hail fell 7%. Yellow cabs rose 9.5%. Traced the 58-point demand gap to transit access, not income. Modelled platform substitution (2.65×) and projected a $130M revenue swing from a proposed 2027 toll increase. Argued transit investment protects low-income riders better than fare exemptions. Published in full on Zenodo.
